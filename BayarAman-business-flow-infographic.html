BayarAman: Rekber Modern di Luar Marketplace

🎯 Fokus: MVP Manual-First + Trust Flow
ℹ️ Buyer bayar ke BayarAman → dana diamankan → seller kirim → buyer konfirmasi → dana diteruskan.
1 ROADMAP FLOW BISNIS UTAMA
1🧾

Seller Buat Link

Nominal, kategori, kesepakatan, deadline, fee payer.

2👀

Buyer Review

Cek detail transaksi, fee, total bayar, policy refund.

3🏦

Buyer Transfer

Transfer manual ke BCA BayarAman.

klik: Sudah Bayar
4🔍

Admin Verifikasi

Cek mutasi, nominal, rekening, anomali.

5🔐

Dana Diamankan

Seller boleh mulai kirim barang/jasa.

6📦

Seller Delivery

Upload resi, foto paket, file, link, atau screenshot.

7✅

Buyer Konfirmasi

Review window 1x24 jam, bisa confirm atau dispute.

8💸

Payout Seller

Finance transfer manual setelah completed.

⭐ Target: buyer berani bayar duluan, seller merasa fair, dispute punya jalur bukti yang jelas.
